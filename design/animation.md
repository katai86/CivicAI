# Animation Strategy

## Microinteractions
- Button hover glow: 150–200ms
- Card lift on hover: 180ms
- Badge hover aura: 180ms

## Map Markers
- Pothole: soft pulsing
- Streetlight: subtle blink
- Tree: green aura

## XP Reward
- Bounce scale-in + confetti burst
- 250ms in, 200ms out

## Intro
- Logo fade-in 0.8s
- City glow pulse 1.2s
- Fade out to map 1.1s

## Accessibility
- `prefers-reduced-motion` disables pulses
